EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

1. PARTIES AND EFFECTIVE DATE

This Employment Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into effective January 1, 2024, by and between Acme Corp, a corporation organized under the laws of Delaware ("Employer"), and John Doe, an individual ("Employee").

2. TERM OF EMPLOYMENT

2.1 Term. The term of this Agreement shall commence on January 1, 2024 and continue for a period of twenty-four (24) months, unless earlier terminated pursuant to Section 4 below.

2.2 Renewal. This Agreement may be renewed upon mutual written consent of both parties.

3. COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS

3.1 Base Salary. Employee shall receive an annual salary of Eighty Thousand Dollars ($80,000.00), payable in accordance with Employer's standard payroll practices.

3.2 Bonus. Employee shall be eligible to receive an annual performance bonus of up to 20% of base salary, subject to achievement of performance metrics as determined by Employer.

3.3 Benefits. Employee shall be entitled to all standard benefits including health insurance, 401(k) participation, and paid time off as per Company policy.

4. TERMINATION

4.1 Termination for Cause. Employer may terminate this Agreement for Cause (as defined below) upon five (5) days' written notice. "Cause" means: (a) material breach of this Agreement; (b) gross negligence or willful misconduct; (c) conviction of a felony; or (d) unauthorized absence exceeding three (3) consecutive business days.

4.2 Termination Without Cause. Employer may terminate this Agreement without Cause upon thirty (30) days' written notice. Employee shall receive severance pay equal to three (3) months' base salary.

4.3 Resignation. Employee may resign at any time upon thirty (30) days' written notice.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY AND NON-DISCLOSURE

5.1 Confidential Information. Employee acknowledges that during employment, Employee will have access to Confidential Information, including trade secrets, proprietary processes, client lists, and financial information.

5.2 Obligation. Employee agrees to maintain in strict confidence all Confidential Information and shall not disclose such information to any third party without prior written consent of Employer.

5.3 Post-Employment. The confidentiality obligation shall survive termination of employment for a period of three (3) years.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

6.1 Assignment. All inventions, works, discoveries, and improvements created by Employee during the term of employment shall be the exclusive property of Employer.

6.2 Work Made for Hire. All such creations shall be considered "works made for hire" under applicable law.

7. NON-COMPETITION AND NON-SOLICITATION

7.1 Non-Competition. During the term of employment and for a period of twelve (12) months following termination, Employee shall not engage in any business that directly competes with Employer's business within a 50-mile radius of Employer's principal place of business.

7.2 Non-Solicitation. Employee shall not solicit Employer's clients or employees for a period of twelve (12) months following termination.

8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

8.1 Arbitration. Any dispute arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the American Arbitration Association rules.

8.2 Venue. The arbitration shall take place in New York County, New York.

9. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York, without regard to its conflict of law provisions.

10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations and agreements.
