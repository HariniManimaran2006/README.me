RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

1. PARTIES

This Lease Agreement is made and entered into on January 15, 2024, between PropertyCorp LLC, a Limited Liability Corporation ("Landlord"), and Jane Smith, an individual ("Tenant").

2. PROPERTY

Landlord hereby leases to Tenant the residential property located at 123 Main Street, New York, NY 10001 (the "Property").

3. LEASE TERM

3.1 The lease term shall be for twelve (12) months, commencing on February 1, 2024 and ending on January 31, 2025.

3.2 Auto-Renewal. This lease shall automatically renew for successive one-year periods unless either party provides sixty (60) days' written notice of non-renewal prior to the end of the then-current term.

4. RENT AND SECURITY DEPOSIT

4.1 Monthly Rent. Tenant agrees to pay Landlord monthly rent in the amount of Two Thousand Dollars ($2,000.00), due on the first day of each month.

4.2 Late Fees. If rent is not received by the 5th day of the month, Tenant shall pay a late fee of $200.00.

4.3 Security Deposit. Tenant shall deposit Two Thousand Dollars ($2,000.00) as security. This deposit shall be returned within thirty (30) days of lease termination, less any deductions for damages.

5. USE OF PROPERTY

5.1 The Property shall be used solely as a private residence.

5.2 No illegal activity shall be conducted on the Property.

5.3 Pets are not allowed without prior written consent.

6. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS

6.1 Landlord Responsibilities. Landlord shall maintain the structural integrity, roof, plumbing, and electrical systems in good working order.

6.2 Tenant Responsibilities. Tenant shall maintain the interior of the Property in good condition and report any maintenance issues promptly.

7. TERMINATION

7.1 Early Termination. If Tenant terminates early without cause, Tenant shall forfeit the security deposit and pay a termination fee equal to three (3) months' rent.

7.2 Landlord Termination. Landlord may terminate for non-payment of rent after providing ten (10) days' notice.

8. GOVERNING LAW

This Lease shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.
